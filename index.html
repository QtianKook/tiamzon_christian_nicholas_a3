A cigarette Kiss (I hide my intention )

By Christian Nicholas C. Tiamozn

Come closer (but not too close)
let the flame of my cigarette tip
pass onto you (swallow you WHOLLY
but… slowly) do not quiver as you feel
something between the filter and
your lips (embrace its warmth like it was mine).

Now distance yourself (just not
toofar) feel the sensation draw inside
(remember all your worries) inhale it
a product of my love (a relaxing but
subtle poison for us both) we are intertwined
together (may death do us part).
